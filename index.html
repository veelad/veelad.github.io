XC2 Companion — Support

XC2 Companion is a fan-made checklist and progress tracker for Xenoblade Chronicles 2 players. This page provides support information for the iOS and Android app.

Contact

For help, bug reports, or feature requests, email veelected@gmail.com. We typically reply within 2–3 business days.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reset my checklist progress?

Open the app, go to the category you want to reset, and use the menu (top right) to clear checked items. A full-app reset can be done from the device's Settings app under the app's storage.

My purchases aren't showing after reinstall.

Open the app's Settings screen and tap Restore Purchases. You must be signed in with the same Apple ID or Google account that made the original purchase.

Ads aren't loading / the app shows a blank banner area.

Make sure you have an active internet connection. Ads may take a few seconds to load on first launch. Ad inventory can vary by region.

How do I request a refund?

Refunds for in-app purchases are handled by Apple or Google. Visit reportaproblem.apple.com (iOS) or play.google.com/store/account/orderhistory (Android).

Does the app require an account?

No. Progress is stored locally on your device. No sign-in is required.

Will the app sync across devices?

Not currently. Each device maintains its own progress. Cloud sync is a planned feature.

I found a bug or have a suggestion.

Please email veelected@gmail.com with a short description, the device you're on, and steps to reproduce if applicable.

Privacy

The app stores your checklist progress locally on your device. It does not collect personal information or require an account. If you consent to tracking when prompted, ad identifiers may be used to deliver personalized ads; otherwise only non-personalized ads are shown. For details, see our Privacy Policy.
