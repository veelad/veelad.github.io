Privacy Policy — XCX Companion

Last updated: April 28, 2026

XCX Companion ("the App") is a free, offline reference app for Xenoblade Chronicles X. This policy explains what data the App does and does not collect.

Data We Collect

The App does not collect, transmit, or share any personal information. Specifically:

  • No account or login is required.
  • No usage analytics or crash reporting data is sent to any server.
  • No advertising SDKs are included.
  • No location, camera, microphone, or contact data is accessed.

Local Storage

The App stores your checklist progress locally on your device using the operating system's standard storage APIs. This data never leaves your device.

Third-Party Services

The App does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy is updated, the revised version will be posted at this URL with a new "Last updated" date.
